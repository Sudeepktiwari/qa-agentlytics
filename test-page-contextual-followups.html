üß™ Testing Instructions

How to Test:
  1. Visit Page: Go to any of your website pages with rich content
  2. Wait for Followup #1: Should extract and reference specific features from page
  3. Interact: Click a button or type a response
  4. Wait for Followup #2: Should reference DIFFERENT page content/features
  5. Interact: Click another button or respond
  6. Wait for Followup #3: Should reference yet DIFFERENT page content/features

‚úÖ Success Criteria:

  • Content Extraction: Each followup references actual features/services from the page
  • Topic Diversification: Each followup explores different business aspect
  • Button Relevance: Buttons use real terminology found on the page
  • Customer Profiling: Questions reveal different business needs and priorities
  • No Repetition: No overlap in concepts, features, or terminology between followups

‚ùå Failure Indicators:

  • Generic messages like "Tired of scheduling chaos" not based on page content
  • Buttons with generic terms not found on the actual page
  • Repetition of same business aspect across multiple followups
  • Messages that could apply to any business, not specific to page content

üéØ Customer Profiling Strategy

Each followup should build a comprehensive customer profile by extracting different aspects from page content:

Followup #1: Business Operations & Core Features
Followup #2: Revenue & Customer Experience
Followup #3: Analytics & Strategic Growth
Result: By the end of 3 followups, you should understand:
  • What features they're most interested in (from page content)
  • Their primary business challenges (extracted from page pain points)
  • Their business priorities (operations vs revenue vs growth)
  • Their technical sophistication (based on feature complexity chosen)
  • Their decision-making stage (based on interaction patterns)
